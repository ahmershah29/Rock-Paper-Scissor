Getting Started

1
Allow camera access when prompted to enable hand detection.
2
For each gesture (Rock, Paper, etc.), click its button 15-20 times while showing that gesture to the camera.
3
Hold your hand in the center of the camera view for best results.
4
Click the "Train Network" button to create your personalized gesture recognition model.

Playing the Game

1
After training, click "Start Game" to begin playing against the AI.
2
Show your gesture to the camera and hold it steady.
3
The AI will make its move and the results will be shown below.
4
Your score will be tracked and saved to the leaderboard.

Camera Issues

1
Ensure your browser has permission to access your camera.
2
Try using Chrome or Firefox for best compatibility.
3
Make sure no other applications are using your camera.
4
Ensure you have adequate lighting for your camera to see your hand clearly.

Model Training

1
Collect at least 15 samples for each gesture you want to use.
2
If recognition is poor, try "Add More Samples" to refine the model.
3
Keep your hand position consistent for each gesture during training.
4
You can save your trained model using the "Save Model" button for future use.

Loading Models

1
If "Load Model" doesn't work automatically, select both the .json and .weights.bin files when prompted.
2
Your model is saved in your browser and should load automatically in future sessions.
